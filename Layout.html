Proposed Floor Plan Layout

1. Entry & Utility

  • Garage (2-car) → connects to Mudroom (~10×12 ft) with bench, cabinets, and countertop for groceries
  • Laundry Room nearby, separate from mudroom

2. Public / Common Areas

  • Kitchen with adjacent breakfast/sunroom
  • Pantry nearby
  • Optional powder room for guests (~6×6 ft) near kitchen/living areas
  • Formal Dining Room (~12×18 ft) Seats 8, adjustable eye-level lighting
  • Living / Family Room with fireplace, large windows, or deck access

3. Audio & Entertainment

  • JBL Listening Room (~18×20 ft) with meditation corner, acoustic treatment, diffusers, velour curtains
  • Home Theater (~20×25 ft) with 95–100″ OLED, surround system, Stickley/Mission-style furniture, fireplace, optional red ambient lighting, optimized ceiling angulation for acoustics

4. Wellness Suite

  • Indoor Endless Pool / Grotto Room (~20–25 ft × 12–16 ft) with underwater stereo, seating niches, LED accent lighting
  • Sauna adjacent (~6–8 ft × 6–8 ft)
  • Stretch / Yoga / Martial Arts Area (~12–15 ft square)
  • Dedicated restroom adjacent to wellness suite for convenience (~6×8 ft)

5. Music & Gym

  • Music Room (~18×20 ft) with piano, Rhodes, guitars, sax storage, recording equipment, felt-backed wood panels, low cabinets
  • Home Gym (~20×24 ft) with weight machines, cardio equipment, Lemond RevMaster, open mat area for stretching/Yoga/Aikido
  • Optional adjacent bathroom / changing area (~6×8 ft)

6. Bedrooms

  • Master Suite (~18–20 ft × 18–20 ft) with queen bed, fireplace, meditation corner, deck access
  • Walk-in Closet (~10–12×12–14 ft)
  • En-suite Bath with shower/tub, vanity, WC
  • Partner / Guest Bedroom (~14–16 ft × 14–16 ft) with walk-in closet, en-suite bath with walk-in shower, single vanity, WC
  • Guest Bedroom (~12×14 ft) with reach-in closet, near common bath (~6×8 ft)

7. Outdoor / Auxiliary

  • Wrap-around decks with partially covered areas for storm and snow viewing
  • Greenhouse attached or separate, white & forest green, with airflow management
  • Outdoor fireplace, barbecue, and stereo zones

8. Toileting / Powder Room Additions

  • Near kitchen / living area: guest powder room (~6×6 ft)
  • Near gym / wellness suite: shower/toilet (~6×8 ft)
  • Optional office powder room (~5×6 ft)
